Frequently Asked Questions

What are microgreens?

Microgreens are young vegetable greens harvested just after the first leaves develop. They’re nutrient-dense and flavorful.

How long do microgreens stay fresh?

They typically stay fresh for up to 7 days when kept refrigerated in a sealed container.

Do you use any chemicals or pesticides?

Nope. Mikro microgreens are grown with only organic soil, non-GMO seeds, and filtered water.
